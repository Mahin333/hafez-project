About Salah

Salah is the second of the Five Pillars of Islam. It is the belief that Muslims should pray five times each day. Prayer is important as it allows Muslims to communicate with Allah, listen to Allah and follow in the footsteps of the prophets. Prayer is performed five times each day because of what the Qur’an says about prayer: Establish prayer at the decline of the sun [from its meridian] until the darkness of the night and [also] the Qur’an of dawn. Indeed, the recitation of dawn is ever witnessed. (Qur'an 17:78).

Shahadah is the belief that “there is no God but Allah- and Muhammad is his messenger”. All people converting to Islam must say these words to become part of the Islamic faith.
